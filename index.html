About Me

I am Sasan Ghanbari Asl (M.Sc., Sharif University of Technology). My background spans chemical engineering and biomedical engineering with a focus on translational biomaterials. I have experience designing and characterizing ECM-based scaffolds and microcarriers for cardiac tissue engineering and am currently seeking PhD opportunities to combine biomaterials with targeted delivery approaches (e.g., transient mRNA delivery to immune cells).

Research Interests

  • Tissue engineering and regenerative medicine
  • Drug & gene delivery (transient mRNA delivery concepts)
  • Extracellular matrix (ECM) scaffolds and decellularized biomaterials
  • Design and characterization of porous microcarriers and hydrogels
  • Translational biomaterials and ex vivo cell conditioning
